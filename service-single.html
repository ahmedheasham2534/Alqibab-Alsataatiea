Home Construction

Home construction is the process of building a residential structure that combines functionality, comfort, and durability. It begins with planning and design, where architects and engineers create detailed blueprints tailored to the homeowner's needs. Site preparation follows, involving excavation, leveling, and foundation work.

Structural framing provides the framework for walls, roofs, and floors, while plumbing, electrical, and HVAC systems ensure functionality.
